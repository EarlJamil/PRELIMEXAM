Company Profile

Vogue Salon has been at the forefront of beauty and hair care since 1990, setting the standard for elegance, innovation, and client satisfaction. Founded by Precious, Kim, and Earl, the salon began as a shared dream to elevate the salon experience through artistry, precision, and heartfelt service.

Over the years, Vogue Salon has grown into a trusted brand with a loyal clientele, a talented team of stylists, and a reputation for excellence. From classic cuts to avant-garde transformations, every service is delivered with care, creativity, and a deep understanding of individual beauty.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the leading salon brand known for redefining beauty through innovation, inclusivity, and exceptional service. We envision a world where every individual feels confident, empowered, and celebrated for their unique style.

Our Mission

To deliver outstanding beauty experiences by combining expert craftsmanship, personalized care, and a commitment to excellence. We strive to create a welcoming space where creativity thrives and every guest leaves feeling transformed and inspired.

Meet the Founders

Precious, Kim, and Earl are the creative minds behind Vogue Salon. United by a shared passion for beauty, innovation, and empowerment, they transformed their vision into a thriving salon experience. Each founder brings a unique strength, Precious with her eye for elegance, Kim with her flair for trendsetting styles, and Earl with his commitment to precision and client care.

Together, they’ve built a legacy of excellence, inspiring a team of stylists and earning the trust of a loyal community. Vogue Salon isn’t just a brand, it’s a reflection of their artistry, dedication, and belief in the power of transformation.
